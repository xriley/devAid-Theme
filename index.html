Redmond STEM Center

An upcoming community youth makerspace in Redmond, WA

PROJECT PROPOSAL BUSINESS PLAN
We're a group of high school students raising funds for a youth community maker space in the Redmond, WA area.
Some examples of activities that will be at the Redmond STEM Center are:

  • Host FIRST Robotics Team 7461

    Founded in 2018, this award-winning Robotics team is open to all high school students, competing in the FIRST robotics competition. You can learn more on the team's website.website.

  • Phoenix Rocketry Organization

    Phoenix Rocketry Organization is a nonprofit team of dedicated high school students who are a regular participant in the American Rocketry Challenge (TARC), the world's largest model rocketry competition.

  • Open hack nights

    One night a week, the center will allow anybody, even non members, to use the tools under adult supervision.

  • Host classes

    With connections to industry professionals, RSC will host classes in programming, CAD (computer aided design), shop tools, electronics, and more.

  • Create new clubs and teams

    In addition to the already existing robotics and rocketry teams, RSC will create and host more clubs and teams.
